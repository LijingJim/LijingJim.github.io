I am a Ph.D. candidate in Mechanical Engineering at **The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)**. My current research focuses on **operation-aware aircraft conceptual design**, **airline fleet planning**, and **learning-based optimization** for sustainable aviation. I work with **Prof. Rhea P. Liem** and collaborate across aerospace design, operations research, and machine learning.

Before starting my Ph.D., I completed an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering at HKUST and a B.Eng. in Mechanical Engineering at Wuhan University of Technology. My recent projects span reinforcement-learning-based fleet sizing and allocation, data-driven aircraft design optimization, and adaptive computational resource scheduling in collaboration with Huawei Hong Kong Research Center.

Research Interests
======

* Aircraft design, multidisciplinary design and optimization, and mission analysis
* Machine learning, surrogate modeling, and reinforcement learning for engineering systems
* Operations research, scheduling, and resource optimization

Current Work
======

* **Operation-driven fleet sizing and allocation optimization** for short-term aircraft leasing using hierarchical multi-agent reinforcement learning.
* **Operation-aware aircraft conceptual design optimization** using data-driven methods to better connect aircraft design decisions with airline operations.
* **Adaptive computational resource scheduling** for multi-core systems through dependency decomposition and optimization.

Selected Publications

1. **Bilevel Strategy for On-Design eVTOL Sizing and Off-Design Structural Weight Optimization**. ICAS 2026, accepted.
2. **Co-design of Aircraft Configuration and Flight Profile for Match-range Operation**. *Aeronautics and Astronautics*, accepted, 2025.
3. **Operation-based Flight Profile Optimization with Continuous Cruise Climb Operations**. AIAA Aviation 2025 Forum.
