Adaptive Computational Resource Scheduling via Dependency Decomposition
Multi-core CPU scheduling optimization through temporal DAG decomposition
📅 Period 10/2024 – 04/2025 🏢 Partner Huawei 2012 Lab HK ⚡ Status Completed
